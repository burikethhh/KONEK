How It Works

Three Steps to Emergency Response

From SOS to dispatch in seconds — even without internet

01

Citizen Sends SOS

Press the SOS button on the KONEK app or edge node. Fill in your emergency type, location, and details. Works offline via LoRa mesh.

SOS
02

LoRa Mesh Routes It

The SOS packet hops through edge nodes and relay nodes across the mesh. Up to 5 hops, no internet required.

03

MDRRMO Responds

The dashboard receives the alert in real-time. Operators acknowledge, dispatch responders, and coordinate multi-agency action.

Features

Everything for Disaster Response

A complete platform from citizen alert to multi-agency coordination

Offline SOS

Send emergency alerts without internet or cellular. LoRa mesh ensures your call for help always gets through.

Real-time Dashboard

Live map with GPS-tagged SOS markers, status tracking, and instant WebSocket updates for MDRRMO operators.

Multi-Agency

MDRRMO, BFP, PNP, RESCUE — each agency sees their assigned incidents with role-based access control.

Broadcast Alerts

Send emergency warnings to all app users instantly. Weather alerts, evacuation notices, and safety instructions.

Incident Reports

Community-submitted incident reports with photo evidence, GPS tagging, and verification workflow for operators.

Environmental Monitoring

Real-time weather, seismic activity, air quality, fault lines, and tropical cyclone tracking for proactive response.

Captive Portal

Edge nodes create a WiFi hotspot with auto-opening SOS form. No app needed — just connect and submit.

Resilient Mesh

Self-healing network with relay nodes extending coverage. Packets auto-route around failures across the mesh.
